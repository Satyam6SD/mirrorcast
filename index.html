Get started
Up and running in 2 minutes

Four simple steps, then you're live

1
Enable USB Debugging on your phone
Go to Settings → About Phone → tap Build Number 7 times → go back → Developer Options → turn on USB Debugging
2
Download and install MirrorCast
Download the installer below, double click it and follow the setup wizard. Takes less than a minute.
3
Plug in your USB cable
Connect your Android phone to your PC with your charging cable. A dialog will appear on your phone — tap Allow.
4
You're live!
MirrorCast automatically detects your phone and starts mirroring. Your phone screen appears on your PC instantly.
